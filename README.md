3. **Choose a font** by tapping Font and selecting from the list

## FAQ

### General

**Q: Does the app require an internet connection?**  
A: No. LED Aura Board works completely offline. All your data is stored locally on your device.

**Q: Can I use emojis in my banner?**  
A: Yes! You can include emojis in your text, though display may vary by font.

### Display

**Q: How do I exit full-screen mode?**  
A: Simply tap anywhere on the screen to exit and return to the editor.

**Q: Can I keep the screen on while displaying?**  
A: Yes, the app prevents screen sleep while in full-screen display mode.

### Data & Privacy

**Q: Where is my data stored?**  
A: All your banners, presets, and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not collect or store any personal data on our servers.

**Q: Does the app track me?**  
A: No. We do not use analytics, cookies, or tracking. See our [Privacy Policy](privacy-policy.md) for details.

## Troubleshooting

**App crashes or freezes**
- Force close the app and reopen
- Restart your device
- Ensure you have the latest app version installed

**Text not displaying correctly**
- Try a different font
- Reduce text length if using large font sizes

**Animation is choppy**
- Close other apps to free up memory
- Reduce the overlay density setting
